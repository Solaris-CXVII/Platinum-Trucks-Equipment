About Platinum Trucks & Equipment

When it comes to experience in the Japanese auto industry, PLatinum Trucks & Equipment has more than just enough to deliver the used vehicle ideal to its valued customers. Its mission is to provide customers with the high quality used vehicles at great prices,with excellent customer service.

The company is dedicated in providing quick online solutions in selling vehicles for personal and business needs. In addition, it’s a member of the popular “Car Auctioneers in Japan”.

With its years of experience, mission, and connections, Platinum Trucks & Equipment, guarantees its customers the best experience in delivering ideal used vehicles.

"YOUR TRUSTED COMPANY
WE DELIVER, WE MAKE IT HAPPEN."

Platinum Trucks & Equipment specializes in selling and shipping used vehicles from Japan to the Philippines. It was established in 2008, with its main office located at Oomiya, Saitama, Japan.

The company’s founder knew that there had to be a better way to purchase used vehicles from Japan. And so, the company was formed and started its selling and shipping business.

Over the past decade, Platinum Trucks & Equipment has stayed within the industry and has grown to offer a wide selection of used cars, trucks, and heavy equipment to choose from.
